January 6th, 2011: T-Rex's Green Lantern Ring Potential Equation, Featuring Drake:

N = R* × fp × ne × fl × fi × fg × L × Cp

where:

N = the expected number of Green Lantern Power Rings on your fingers

and

R* = the average rate of star formation per year in our galaxy
fp = the fraction of those stars that have planets
ne = the average number of planets that can potentially support life per star that has planets
fl = the fraction of the above that actually go on to develop life at some point
fi = the fraction of the above that actually go on to develop intelligent life
fg = the fraction of civilizations that develop Green Lantern technology and that release detectable Green Lantern Corps into space
L = the length of time for which such Green Lantern Corps survive
Cp = your personal charisma, ranging from 0 (least charismatic person) to 1 (perfectly charismatic)

For more information on T-Rex's Green Lantern Potential Equation, Featuring Drake, visit Wikipedia's page on the Drake Equation and make the appropriate Drake / T-Rex name substitutions wherever appropriate.
